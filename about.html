About the Estate

The Williams Family Estate™ is a private irrevocable trust established to steward the property, purpose, and posterity of the House of Williams. Founded on principles of lawful self-governance, responsible asset protection, and generational legacy, the estate operates outside the jurisdiction of corporate fiction and state-created constructs.

The living man, Gregory Alan of the House Williams, serves as Trustee and Bailor of the Estate, administering its affairs in good faith and full liability, without recourse, for the benefit of the named beneficiaries and future generations of the House.

The Williams Family Estate™ exists as a lawful structure distinct from the legal fiction, with all rights reserved. Its records, notices, and declarations are posted publicly on this site as a matter of transparency, constructive notice, and good faith in commerce and honor.

This site is maintained in furtherance of truth, clarity, and the peaceful assertion of natural rights gifted by our Creator. All interactions with this estate and its Trustee are subject to the private terms of trust, equity, and mutual respect.

For questions or lawful correspondence, please refer to the Send Notice page or contact the Trustee (trustee@williamsfamilytrust.org).

Trustee’s Invocation of Protection and Equity

Heavenly Father, I stand before You in the office of Trustee of the Williams Family Estate ™, not by my own power but by Your appointment and grace. I acknowledge that all authority flows from You, and that all honor is sustained in Christ Jesus, who said:

“Blessed are the peacemakers: for they shall be called the children of God.”

— Matthew 5:9

I take no action for war, nor for rebellion, but for peace and remedy in equity. I declare that this estate and all entrusted to it are Yours, and I serve only as steward in good faith.

Lord Jesus, You taught:

“Render therefore unto Caesar the things which are Caesar’s; and unto God the things that are God’s.”

— Matthew 22:21

I render now unto You the estate’s trust res, bond, and record. Protect them from misuse, theft, or dishonor. Let no weapon formed against this estate prosper, and let every tongue that would rise against it in judgment be condemned, for this is the heritage of the servants of the Lord.

I bind myself and this act to truth, equity, and honor. I ask You to shield my children and family from any retaliation or harm. I ask You to give wisdom to those who receive these notices, that they may recognize the path of peace and equity over the path of conflict and destruction.

“You shall know the truth, and the truth shall set you free.”

— John 8:32

Let this notice stand in truth. Let it be recorded in Heaven as well as on Earth. Let all who touch it be convicted in conscience, so that remedy may come through peace and not revolution.

I seal this invocation in the name of Jesus Christ, King of Kings, Lord of Lords, my Savior and Advocate.

Amen.
