This Public Notice establishes and publishes the official Fee & Penalty Schedule of the Williams Family Estate™. This schedule sets forth the liquidated damages applicable to dishonor, breach, or injury in commerce or equity, including but not limited to refusal of lawful tender, damage to the Estate’s creditworthiness, levy or seizure, interference with beneficiaries, trespass, compelled medical procedures, wrongful death, suppression of notice, and breach of fiduciary duty.

Summary of Key Provisions

  • Dishonor of notices or tenders: $10,000–$250,000 per occurrence.
  • Failure to honor perfected Performance Bond: $100,000 per occurrence.
  • Damage to Estate’s creditworthiness: $500,000 + $10,000/day.
  • Unlawful levy, lien, or seizure of Estate property: $1,000,000 + $25,000/day.
  • Forced vaccination or compelled medical procedure: $2,000,000 + $50,000/day.
  • Wrongful death of beneficiary: $20,000,000 per occurrence.
  • Custody interference with beneficiaries: $5,000,000 per occurrence.
  • Unlawful identity use, forced joinder, or mail tampering: $250,000–$1,000,000 per act.
  • Suppression of Estate notice or breach of privacy: $500,000–$1,000,000 per act.
  • Trespass, conversion, or misappropriation of Estate property: $1,000,000 + restoration.
  • Psychological, spiritual, or moral harm to beneficiaries: $2,000,000 per act.
  • Breach of fiduciary duty or self-dealing by Trustee: $10,000,000 per occurrence, removal from office, and personal liability.

All penalties are immediately due and payable upon dishonor or injury. Failure to cure within ten (10) days constitutes default and consent to enforcement. Remedies include setoff, lien, levy, or commercial claim.

This Fee & Penalty Schedule is published as public notice and constitutes lawful terms of engagement in all dealings with Williams Family Estate™.
