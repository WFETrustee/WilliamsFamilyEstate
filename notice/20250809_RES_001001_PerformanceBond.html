Notice of Trust Performance Bond – WFE-001

Issued: August 1, 2025

Venue: Williams Family Estate™ – Public Notice Record  |  Identifier: WFE-001

Comes now the Trustee, Gregory-Alan: Williams, a living man and Trustee of the private Williams Family Estate™, to give constructive public notice that the Trust Performance Bond identified as WFE-001 has been duly issued and executed under private equity jurisdiction in the amount of $5,000,000 USD, as a lawful guarantee of performance, indemnity, and remedy for all acts undertaken by or through the Estate and the DEBTOR trust res styled GREGORY ALAN WILLIAMS.

Authority & Security

  • Backed by the full faith and credit of the trust res and the Williams Family Estate™ indenture;
  • Secured via UCC‑1 Financing Statement No. 202505068223791 (Secured Party: GREGORY ALAN WILLIAMS TRUST™);
  • Issued pursuant to Trustee’s Durable Limited Power of Attorney and Affidavit of Authority;
  • Executed under hand and official trust seal; certified copies carry the same force as the original.

Guarantees

  • Good‑faith and full performance of all trust and commercial duties undertaken by the Trustee;
  • Equitable discharge and settlement of verified lawful obligations;
  • Indemnity for any man, woman, or lawful entity harmed by dishonor, trespass, or administrative error;
  • Preservation of the value, creditworthiness, and function of the DEBTOR as active trust res.

Terms (Abbreviated)

  • Validity: 99 years or until lawfully revoked and settled;
  • Claim: May be called upon by verified affidavit of injury under penalty of perjury;
  • Rebuttal: 21 days from notice, or terms stand as true, binding, and enforceable;
  • Instrument: Private equity; not legal tender; not subject to U.S. corporate statutes.
