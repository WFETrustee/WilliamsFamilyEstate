Correction to Certificate of Service: IRS Response Package

Filed: July 28, 2025

Venue: Published on the public record of the Williams Family Estate™ – Identifier: IRS-COS002

On July 26, 2025, a response package to a presentment from the IRS was served via USPS Certified Mail with Return Receipt to Commissioner Billy Long at the address of record.

The Certificate of Service included in that mailing contained a clerical oversight: a placeholder reference for the online mailing platform remained in the printed version (stating “[insert name of online service if applicable]”).

Although this omission does not affect the validity of service or the contents of the response package, the Trustee affirms the importance of maintaining the highest standard of integrity and clarity on the public record. The corrected Certificate of Service has been archived internally and is hereby acknowledged publicly.

This correction is made to ensure transparency and trustworthiness in all public-facing communications issued by the Williams Family Estate™.
