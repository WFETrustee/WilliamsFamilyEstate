Instructions & Legal Basis

This document functions as:

  • A living man’s affidavit of status
  • A jurisdictional demand for proof of claim
  • A trigger for judicial review via habeas corpus
  • A commercial conditional acceptance for value

Delivery of this document:

  • Exits the DEBTOR fiction and places burden of proof on the detaining party
  • Triggers production of jurisdictional authority, bond trail, and verified injury
  • Enters into lawful record and becomes standing truth in commerce if unrebutted
  • Holds public officers commercially and personally liable for ultra vires acts

Who May Deliver

  • Next friend
  • Partner
  • Successor trustee
  • Authorized agent (even verbally)

Best Practice Delivery Steps

  1. Print 2 copies: one for Clerk of Court, one for Sheriff or Jail Commander
  2. Instruct Clerk to time-stamp and enter into record, request stamped receipt
  3. Deliver in person to Jail or Sheriff with clear statement of authority
  4. Keep a delivery log or affidavit of service as proof

Final Notes:

  • Ensure notarization prior to emergency use
  • Include trust mailing address, even if c/o physical location
  • (Optionally) include a QR code linking to a backup digital copy
